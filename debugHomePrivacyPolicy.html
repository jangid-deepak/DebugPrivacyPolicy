Privacy Policy for Debug Home Application

Effective Date: November 28, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to the Debug Home application. Your privacy is important to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information. This policy is designed to ensure transparency and provide clarity regarding your data.

Information Collection

The Debug Home application collects information related to bookings, including details of the admin users managing these bookings. The information collected may include but is not limited to:

  • Admin user identification details (e.g., username, email address)
  • Booking details (e.g., customer name, pick-up and drop locations, booking status)

Use of Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of managing and administering bookings. This includes:

  • Viewing, updating, and managing booking details
  • Assigning pick-up and drop agents
  • Changing the status of bookings

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. We implement robust security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information and booking data. These measures include encryption, secure access protocols, and regular security audits.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information or booking data with any third parties. All information collected within the Debug Home application is used exclusively for the purposes outlined in this privacy policy.

User Rights

As an admin user, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal information and booking data
  • Request corrections to any inaccuracies in your data
  • Delete your personal information and booking data, subject to your choice

To delete your data, you can submit a request through our support team (debugonlineindia@gmail.com).

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted within the application, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.

Governing Law

This privacy policy is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of or relating to this policy will be resolved under the jurisdiction of the courts in Jodhpur, Rajasthan, India.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact our support team through the Debug Home application or via email at debugonlineindia@gmail.com.
